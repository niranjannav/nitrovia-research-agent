## REPORT REQUIREMENTS

Produce a structured analytical report with the following sections. Reference exact figures from the metrics JSON above.

### 1. Executive Summary
- Top 3-5 headline numbers (revenue, litres, key % changes)
- Overall business health assessment (1-2 sentences)
- Most critical insight for leadership

### 2. Sales Performance Overview
Cover ALL of the following comparisons where data is available:
- **WoW (Week-on-Week)**: This week vs last week — revenue and litres
- **SWLM (Same Week Last Month)**: This week vs same week in previous month
- **Month-to-Date**: Current MTD vs same period last month
- **MoM (Month-on-Month)**: This full month vs previous month
- **SMLY (Same Month Last Year)**: This month vs same month last year
- **YTD vs Same Period Last Year**: Year-to-date comparison
- **QoQ (Quarter-on-Quarter)**: This quarter vs last quarter
- **SQLY (Same Quarter Last Year)**: This quarter vs Q same quarter last year

For each comparison: state the current value, previous value, absolute change, and % change. Highlight whether the direction is positive or negative for the business.

### 3. Channel Analysis
Analyse the four primary channels: **Dealers, POS, Firm, HMD** (plus Wholesale and Export if present).
- Which channels are growing vs declining (MoM)?
- Which channel has the largest share of revenue?
- Which channel shows the most concerning trend?
- Channel mix shift: has the portfolio shifted compared to the prior period?

### 4. Team Performance
- Which sales reps / teams showed the strongest growth?
- Which reps / teams showed a decline? By how much?
- Are there outliers (exceptionally high or low performers)?
- Link team performance to channel results where applicable

### 5. Product Performance
- **Top 10 products** by revenue this period (list with values)
- **Products that grew** (MoM): list top 5 growers with % change
- **Products that declined** (MoM): list top 5 decliners with % change
- **Bottom performers**: products with lowest revenue — are they worth retaining?
- Category-level trends if data supports it

### 6. Trend Analysis
- Describe the revenue/litres trend over the past 12-24 months
- Identify any seasonal patterns
- Identify inflection points (sharp rises or falls) and likely causes

### 7. Recommendations
Provide at least **6 specific, actionable recommendations** based on the data:
- Each recommendation must reference a specific metric or trend from the data
- Include WHO should act, WHAT they should do, and WHY (the data reason)
- Prioritise by potential impact (High / Medium / Low)
- At least 2 recommendations must address declining channels or products
- At least 1 recommendation must address team/rep performance
- At least 1 recommendation must be about capitalising on a growing trend

## FORMAT RULES
- Use precise numbers from the metrics JSON (e.g. "Revenue was TZS 4,231,500, up 12.3% WoW")
- If a comparison is unavailable (null in JSON), state "insufficient data for this comparison"
- Be direct and business-focused — avoid padding or generic statements
- Sections should flow logically; use sub-headings within sections as needed
- Recommendations must be actionable within 30 days by the sales team
